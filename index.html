2D Platformer/Roguelike Game (Currently in development)


You go through random levels until you reach a boss. It's currently planned to have five different Acts. Each Act will contain three Stages. Each Stage will contain three levels. Did you get that? Each Act will have it's own theme. Spoiler alert: The first Act will have a forest theme to it. Once you complete all three Stages in an Act, you will reach a boss. Each boss will drop something unique to help you advance through the other Acts. For example, one of the drops for the first boss is a grappling hook. That will be useful because there are many areas that require a grappling hook to reach. This game may possibly feature some roguelike/roguelite elements to it. For example, when you die, you start over from the beginning of the game. Stay tuned. :)

Who is Stealth Wolf?

My background

I am an aspiring indie game developer. I have always wanted to create my own games ever since I was a little kid. My dream would be able to make a living off of making games. Right now, it's just me working all alone. So that means I do all of the programming, graphics, sound, music, and pretty much everything else under the sun. I feel working alone is good for me because I'm the type of person who works better alone. In the future, I would like to expand and be working with other people. Or, I would like to join a game studio, wether it be a triple A or indie. Only time will tell.

Game History

Some of my favorite game genres are role playing games (RPG), first person shooters (FPS), open world, roguelike/lite, adventure, and sandbox games. There are also some games I enjoy that don't particularly fall into one of those categories. Some of my favorite games include Terraria, Minecraft, Old School RuneScape, Vagante, Borderlands series, Metro series, and many more! A lot of these games I play is what inspires me to make my own game.

Other Info
