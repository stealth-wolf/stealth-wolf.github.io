My background

Stealth Wolf is an aspiring indie game developer. Stealth Wolf always wanted to create his own games ever since Stealth Wolf was a little kid. Stealth Wolf's dream would be able to make a living off of making games. Some of Stealth Wolf's favorite game genres are role playing games (RPG), first person shooters (FPS), open world, rouge-like/lite, adventure, and sandbox games. There are also some games I enjoy that don't particually fall into one of those categories. A lot of these games Stealth Wolf plays is what inspires him to make his own game.
